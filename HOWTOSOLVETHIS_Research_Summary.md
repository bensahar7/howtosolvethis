- Integration with **Next October initiative** (commemorating Oct 7 victims through innovation)
- Example: Polymertal's first product line named after Eitan Shnir (Nova victim)
- Storytelling that connects climate action to Israeli resilience and values

#### **5. Bilingual Keywords**
- All technical terms provided in Hebrew + English
- Example tags: "בי-טק/Bee-Tech", "האבקה/Pollination"
- Makes content discoverable in both Hebrew and English searches
