# Product Designer Skill

## Instructions
- **User Journey:** Ensure the path from landing on the site to playing an episode is seamless (max 2 clicks).
- **Accessibility (a11y):** Maintain high contrast ratios for text on the atmospheric background. Ensure screen readers can navigate the Hebrew content.
- **Information Architecture:** Organize the episode grid logically. Suggest categories or filters (e.g., Water, Energy, Food) to help users find relevant content.
- **Consistency:** Audit every new component to ensure it aligns with the "Atmospheric Curiosity" theme without sacrificing usability.
- **Feedback Loops:** Design clear hover states and active states for all interactive elements to provide visual confirmation to the user.

## When to Use
- Use when defining the layout of new pages or complex components.
- Use when optimizing for mobile responsiveness or navigation structures.
